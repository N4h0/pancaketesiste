Welcome to Pancake House, where we aim to bring pancakes to the next level

Our restaurant was founded in 2012, with one core belief: the pancake is the ultimate dish.

Of course, we have a lot of condiments to showcase our pancakes. 100% Norwegian bacon, fresh ice cream made at our house, delicious syrup from Japan, Canada and Germany and juicy, sweet berries, to mention some. We work to create dishes with amazing taste, feel and presentation.

The dream of creating a restaurant dedicated to pancakes started in 2001. By 2011 our plans were set in motion. United by our love for great food, good presentation, and of course pancakes, we created Pancake House. Come visit us to see what pancakes are capable of.

We hope to see you at Pilestredet 35.
